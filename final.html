    Machine cycle

      Four steps performed for each instruction
    1. Fetch:the next instructionis fetched from cache or ram.

    2. Decode:the instructions are decoded into a form the ALU or FPU can understan.

    3. Execute:the instructions are carried out such as the ALU or FPU performing a computation.

    4. Store: the date or results from the instructions excution are stored in registers or ram
